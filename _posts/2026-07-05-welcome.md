I started this blog to write about quantitative football analysis in a practical way.

Most sports content is opinion-first. I want this site to be model-first, data-first, and transparent about uncertainty.

The topics I plan to cover include:
- football analytics
- betting market structure
- Python workflows
- simple predictive models
- backtesting mistakes
- model calibration and expected value

My goal is not to pretend to have perfect predictions.

My goal is to document how to think more clearly about sports, probability, and decision-making.
