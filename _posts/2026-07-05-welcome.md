I started this blog to write about quantitative football analysis in a practical way.

Most sports content is opinion-first. I want this site to be model-first, data-driven, and transparent about uncertainty.

The topics I plan to cover include:
- football analytics
- betting market structure
- Python workflows
- simple predictive models
- backtesting mistakes
- model calibration and expected value

My goal is not to pretend to have perfect predictions. It is to document how to think more clearly about football, probability, models and betting decision-making.
