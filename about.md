# About

I write about football analytics with a focus on data, betting markets, Python, and statistical thinking.

This site is a place to publish:
- research notes
- model ideas
- coding walkthroughs
- postmortems on what failed
- lessons from testing assumptions with data

## Principles

- Show the method, not just the conclusion
- Be honest about uncertainty
- Prefer reproducible analysis
- Separate evidence from narrative
- Treat betting as probabilistic decision making, not emotion-driven gambling
